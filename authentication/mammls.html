Mammals

Discover various mammals, their diets, and if they can be pets.

Back to All Categories

Lion

Diet: Carnivore

Pet Friendly: No

The lion is known as the "king of the jungle." It is a large, muscular cat with a majestic mane.

Elephant

Diet: Herbivore

Pet Friendly: No

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

Dog

Diet: Omnivore

Pet Friendly: Yes

Dogs are loyal companions and one of the most popular pets worldwide.

Dolphin

Diet: Carnivore

Pet Friendly: No

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful nature.
